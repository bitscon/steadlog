# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

Enable homesteaders to run resilient, data-informed operations through one secure and intuitive platform.

## Users

- Individual homesteaders
- Small family farms
- Planning-oriented operators tracking long-term goals

## Core Problems

- fragmented operational tracking across disconnected tools
- poor visibility into planning, execution, and outcomes
- weak continuity in recurring farm workflows

## Strategic Outcomes

- unified planning and tracking workflows
- improved consistency in operational execution
- stronger decision quality from centralized data
