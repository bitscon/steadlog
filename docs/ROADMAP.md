# Technical/Product Roadmap

## Near Term

- complete baseline governance compliance controls
- maintain secure secret handling and policy enforcement
- keep CI quality gates green on main

## Mid Term

- expand automated test coverage across core features
- strengthen observability and incident diagnostics
- improve architecture contract consistency across modules

## Long Term

- mature platform reliability and deployment safety
- reduce operational risk through stronger automation and standards enforcement

## Dependencies and Risks

| Item | Type | Mitigation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Third-party service changes | External dependency | Contract checks and staged rollout |
| Schema drift | Architecture risk | Migration source-of-truth policy |
| Secret handling failures | Security risk | scanning + rotation runbook |
